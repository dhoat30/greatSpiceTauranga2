Warm up this winter with online ordering from Tauranga's leading ethnic restaurant Great Spice Tandoori Indian Restaurant & Bar.

What better way to warm up this winter than with a delicious curry delivered to your door from Tauranga’s favourite ethnic restaurant Great Spice Tandoori Indian Restaurant & Bar.

On a cold winter evening, not many of us will want to brave the cold to get something dinner. You can order scrumptious and authentic curry delivered to your door from Great Spice Tandoori Indian Restaurant & Bar. “You can order our delicious food with just a click of a button,” says Khem Aryal, owner of Great Spice Tandoori Indian Restaurant & Bar.

Great Spice Tandoori Indian Restaurant & Bar is fully licensed restaurant in Tauranga, known for their dedication excellent customer service and superbly delicious meals.

Nothing beats the warmth and flavour of a good curry. At Great Spice Tandoori Indian Restaurant & Bar they have a range of enjoyable dishes that will be sure to warm you up this winter. Whether you like the kiwi favourite butter chicken or feeling a bit adventurous want to try something much more spicy, Great Spice in Tauranga can do it all! “We only sell food of that are made with the best and freshly cooked ingredients and meet our strict high standards,” adds Khem.

With their easy to use online ordering system, it has never been simpler for you to order something scrumptious from Great Spice Tandoori Indian Restaurant & Bar. Just head on over to their website and click on the ‘Order Online’ button to start your order today!

If you want to dine in, Great Spice Tandoori Indian Restaurant & Bar is perfect for your next special occasion, lunch date, or family dinner. Book your table now and don’t forget to check out their weekly specials.
